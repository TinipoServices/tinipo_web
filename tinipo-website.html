Privacy Policy

Your child's safety and privacy are our top priorities

Child Safety & Protection

At TINIPO, we are committed to creating a safe and secure environment for all children. We understand that parents entrust us with their most precious gifts, and we take this responsibility seriously.

  • All staff undergo comprehensive background checks and child protection training
  • Secure facility with controlled access and professional supervision
  • Emergency procedures and first aid protocols always in place

Information We Collect

We collect only the necessary information to provide excellent service and ensure your child's safety:

  • Child and parent contact information for enrollment and emergency purposes
  • Medical information and special needs to ensure appropriate care
  • Photos and videos for progress tracking (with explicit parental consent)

How We Protect Your Information

  • All personal information is stored securely and accessed only by authorized personnel
  • We never share or sell your information to third parties
  • Photos and videos are shared only with explicit parental permission

Your Rights

As a parent or guardian, you have the right to:

  • Review any information we have about your child
  • Request corrections to any inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent for photos, videos, or communications at any time

Questions about our privacy practices?
Contact us at privacy@tinipo.com or call (555) 123-KIDS
